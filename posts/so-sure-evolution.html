The so-sure app evolution

Our app has evolved pretty rapidly over the past year. Here's a light guide to what's changed in each major release.

Quote

Before we launched we decided to release a small 'Quote' app that simply provided an instant quote and took an email so we could let you know when we were ready

The first release

We had lift off! Our first full version of the app included a guided walkthrough your quote and how social insurance works. Then a few pages of info before creating an account. Nice and simple.

Needs more chat bot

Here we augmented the original guided walkthrough into a chat bot experience, it allowed for deeper explanations of how so-sure works and the direct asking of questions. You can see here also how the original dashboard looked, it stayed like this for quite a while.

Cutting the fat

Aaand the chat bots gone. It was instead replaced by a few pages of explanation on the homepage that was easily skippable. This version of the app was all about getting you insured quickly and efficiently.

Getting some style

We decided it was time to add a little more cohesion to our designs. This version introduced a more unified form layout, as well as breaking out the long single page checkout experience into a more refined step by step flow.

A new dashboard

The hompage was replaced to be more inviting and attractive, with a link to a video explanation. The dashboard has been revamped, the tabs are gone and cards have been added aimed at teaching you how to get the most out of so-sure.

More policies, more fun

You can now have multiple policies on one account. This means new ui elements allowing you to switch between your policies. A tin of paint has been dropped to get the app inline with our company colours.
